FAQ

Find answers to your most pressing questions.

What sizes do you offer?
We offer a wide range of sizes from XS to XXL. Our goal is to ensure that everyone can find the perfect fit. If you have specific sizing needs, please reach out to our customer service team for assistance.
How do I care for my dresses?
To maintain the quality of your dresses, we recommend hand washing in cold water and laying them flat to dry. Avoid bleach and direct sunlight to preserve colors and fabrics.
Do you offer custom dresses?
Yes, we specialize in customized designs tailored to your preferences. You can choose fabrics, colors, and styles to create a dress that truly represents your unique style.

About Vera

Learn more about our story and passion for fashion

Our Story

Vera was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to create beautiful, high-quality summer dresses that celebrate individuality and comfort. What started as a small boutique has grown into a beloved brand known for its customized designs and attention to detail.


Our team of talented designers works closely with each client to create dresses that not only look stunning but feel wonderful to wear. We believe that every woman deserves to have clothing that makes her feel confident and beautiful.


At Vera, we're committed to sustainable practices and ethical manufacturing. All our dresses are made with care and respect for both people and the planet.
